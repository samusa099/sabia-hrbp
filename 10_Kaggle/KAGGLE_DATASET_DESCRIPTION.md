# Sabia Group HRBP Smartwatch Recovery 2026

## Context
This synthetic case follows a Bangladesh-based business that invested in smartwatch manufacturing without validating an MVP or prototype. The company experienced defects, rework, overtime, workforce mismatch and operating loss. A strategic HRBP-led programme used feasibility analysis, a controlled pilot, scale-up and enterprise rollout to recover the business.

## Content
The dataset contains linked HR, production and financial tables for Q1-Q4 2026.
