# Ethics and Limitations

## Synthetic data

All employee, company, production, and financial records are fictional.

## Responsible restructuring analysis

Do not use protected characteristics such as gender, age, religion, disability, pregnancy, ethnicity, or health status in employment decisions.

Real restructuring requires:

- verified business need;
- documented role criteria;
- applicable labour-law review;
- fair communication and appeal;
- separation support;
- adverse-impact assessment;
- knowledge-transfer planning.

## Analytical limitations

- synthetic associations do not establish causation;
- pilot results are educational and sample-size dependent;
- the project is not a real company audit;
- the dataset should not be presented as confidential business evidence.
