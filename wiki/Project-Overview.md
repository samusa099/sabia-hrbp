# Project Overview

## Business problem

Sabia Group made a heavy investment in a Made-in-Bangladesh smartwatch operation but entered production without validating an MVP or prototype. The business experienced:

- low first-pass yield;
- high defect, rework, and warranty exposure;
- excessive overtime;
- duplicated or mismatched roles;
- scarce precision-manufacturing capability;
- fragmented HR and production data;
- recurring operating loss.

## HRBP mandate

The central role is a **Lead HR Business Partner who also acts as Head of HR / Acting CHRO**. The mandate is broader than routine HR operations:

- diagnose people-related causes of business loss;
- redesign the organization;
- plan a responsible workforce reset;
- hire scarce technical capability;
- build certification and performance systems;
- introduce HR services and technology;
- prove results in a controlled pilot;
- scale only after evidence.
