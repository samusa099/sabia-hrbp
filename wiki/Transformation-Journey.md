# Q1–Q4 Transformation Journey

## Q1 — Plan and Reset

- feasibility study;
- baseline workforce and production diagnosis;
- KPI catalogue;
- business and workforce scenarios;
- 48-role workforce reset from a 100-person baseline;
- legal, ethical, and knowledge-risk review;
- approval for a 25-person pilot.

## Q2 — Controlled Pilot

- Line A test and control groups;
- precision-assembly and ESD certification;
- quality-at-source;
- supervisor KPI check-ins;
- HR-service ticket SLA;
- HRIS self-service;
- pilot-versus-control evaluation.

## Q3 — Scale and Stabilize

- two-line production scale;
- critical-skill hiring;
- manager dashboards;
- recognition and internal mobility;
- weekly people-production review;
- enterprise-rollout recommendation.

## Q4 — Full Rollout

- group-wide HR operating model;
- integrated HR, production, and financial reporting;
- benefits-realization review;
- governance handover;
- board approval.

## Endpoint outcomes

- Q4 active headcount: 114;
- Q4 endpoint first-pass yield: 97.1%;
- Q4 endpoint defect rate: 2.4%;
- Q4 operating profit: BDT 64.02 million.
